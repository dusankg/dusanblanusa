Computer Vision for Robots

I developed a computer vision system for robots, designed for object recognition and localization. This involved the creation of a custom YOLO v3 convolutional neural network, where I made the entire pipeline, from capturing images for the dataset and labeling them to training the model, testing, and integrating robots software. I used also OpenCV and Python in general for various aspects of the project.
The primary goal of this system was to find cup-like objects within diverse scenarios. Furthermore, I was working on algorithms for robot movements based on the location of these cups.
In addition to this project, I also wrote my bachelor's thesis "Application of convolutional neural networks for object detection in robotics".
